EASON LEE

About me

UI/UX Designer

Eason Lee is coming from a background of Chemical Engineering. He has three years working experience of quality assurance engineer focused on customer service. However, Eason has always had ideas to develop a new product and was filled with interest in design. Therefore, he quitted his job in 2011, left his hometown Taipei, and decided to pursue the MFA degree of Web Design & New Media at Academy of Art University in San Francisco, which is corresponding to his interest and provides an access to fulfill his ideas through designing. Based on his science background and working experience, Eason can solve problems in a logical, efficient and organized way. Besides, he got the first rank of service evaluation from Panasonic when working on AU Optronics, which can contribute him to understand the needs of users when doing the research of User Experience.

Eason Lee is familiar with the latest design trend and methodology. He is proficient in Adobe Creative Suite, Figma, Sketch and front-end coding. He has helped many startups and businesses to improve their products and specialized in GUI Design and UX Design.
