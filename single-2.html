GOMORE REBRANDING

Client: GoMore
Role: Visaul Designer
Brand Design, Rebranding
2016

GoMore was founded in 2011, by a group of sports enthusiasts and data scientists, with high interests in understanding sports performance. The original branding is not practical to use as the business grows, so we conducted a rebranding to align our mind identity and came up with colors based on it. We also defined our new typography accroding to the analysis of the logo.
