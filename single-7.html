PLEASE BE CLOSER

Client: Sofu
Role: Contract Designer
T-shirt Design
2010

This pattern was chosen and produced by SOFU online clothing company. I used this image—the line of dominos fails if people are too far apart— to encourage people shorten the gap between each other and contact with friends more often.
